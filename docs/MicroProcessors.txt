Currently supported Microprocessors
===================================

ia32
====

* 32 (bit protected mode).

* Commonly known as i386, but from a software perspective, encompasses the
  entire Intel range of x86 microprocessors.

* The processor type can be fine tuned by changing the CPU flag in the
  configuration files located in the <base>/config sub-directory.

* All processor specific functions are located in <base>/cpu/ia32
  sub-directory.
